Data Deletion Instructions - Neural Beats Studio

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

Important Notice:
Neural Beats Studio is a desktop application that processes all data locally on your device. We do NOT store your data on our servers. All data deletion is handled locally by you, the user.

How to Delete Your Data

Neural Beats Studio respects your privacy and provides you with full control over your data. All data is stored locally on your device, and you can delete it at any time.

Step-by-Step Data Deletion Guide

1. Delete Local Application Data

Generated Content:

  • Navigate to the output/ directory in your project folder
  • Delete all files and folders to remove generated music and videos
  • Or delete the entire output/ directory

Authentication Data:

  • Delete token.json file (contains OAuth tokens)
  • Delete credentials.json file (contains API credentials)
  • These files are located in your project root directory

Application Files:

  • Uninstall the application to remove all local data
  • Delete the entire project folder if you want to remove everything

2. Revoke Social Media Platform Access

YouTube:

  1. Go to: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
  2. Find "Neural Beats Studio" or your app
  3. Click "Remove Access" or "Revoke Access"

Instagram/Facebook:

  1. Go to: https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=applications
  2. Find "Neural Beats Studio" app
  3. Click "Remove" or "Delete App"

TikTok:

  1. Go to: https://www.tiktok.com/setting/privacy
  2. Navigate to "Connected Apps"
  3. Find "Neural Beats Studio" and revoke access

Spotify:

  1. Go to: https://www.spotify.com/account/apps/
  2. Find "Neural Beats Studio"
  3. Click "Remove Access"

3. Request Data Deletion via Email

If you need assistance with data deletion or have questions, please contact us:

Email: neuralbeats20@gmail.com
Subject: "Data Deletion Request"

Include in your email:
  • Your name
  • Email address associated with the account
  • Any relevant account information
  • Specific data you want deleted
Response Time: We will respond within 7 business days and complete deletion within 30 days.

For Facebook/Instagram Users

If you have connected your Facebook or Instagram account to Neural Beats Studio:

  1. Revoke App Access:
    • Go to: https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=applications
    • Find "Neural Beats Studio" app
    • Click "Remove" or "Delete App"
  2. Local Data:
    • All data is stored locally on your device
    • Delete token.json file to remove OAuth tokens
    • Delete output/ directory to remove generated content
  3. No Server Data:
    • We do NOT store your data on our servers
    • No data deletion request is needed from our side
    • All data is on your local device

What Data Gets Deleted?

Immediately Deleted:

  • ✅ Local files in output/ directory
  • ✅ OAuth tokens in token.json
  • ✅ API credentials in credentials.json

Platform-Specific:

  • YouTube: Videos remain on YouTube unless you delete them manually
  • Instagram: Posts remain on Instagram unless you delete them manually
  • Facebook: Posts remain on Facebook unless you delete them manually
  • TikTok: Videos remain on TikTok unless you delete them manually
  • Spotify: Content remains on Spotify unless you delete it manually

Note: Deleting data from our application does NOT automatically delete content you've uploaded to social media platforms. You must delete that content separately through each platform's interface.

Data Deletion Timeline

  • Local Files: Deleted immediately when you delete them
  • OAuth Tokens: Revoked immediately when you revoke access through platform settings
  • Email Requests: Processed within 7 business days, completed within 30 days
  • Third-Party Platform Data: Subject to each platform's deletion policies (may take up to 90 days)

Verification

After deletion, you can verify:

  • ✅ Local files are removed from output/ directory
  • ✅ OAuth tokens are revoked (check platform settings)
  • ✅ No token.json or credentials.json files remain
