Privacy Policy - Neural Beats Studio

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

1. Introduction

Neural Beats Studio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered music generation and social media upload services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Music Files: Audio files you upload for processing or social media distribution
  • Image Files: Visual content you upload for video creation
  • Metadata: Song titles, descriptions, tags, and genre information
  • Social Media Credentials: OAuth tokens for YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and Spotify (stored locally on your device)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the application (features accessed, upload frequency)
  • Technical Data: System information, device type, operating system (for troubleshooting purposes)
  • Error Logs: Application errors and debugging information (stored locally)

2.3 Third-Party Services

  • Google YouTube API: We use YouTube Data API v3 for video uploads. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Meta/Facebook APIs: For Instagram and Facebook integration. Meta's privacy policy applies: https://www.facebook.com/privacy/explanation
  • TikTok API: For TikTok video uploads. TikTok's privacy policy applies: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Spotify API: For music distribution. Spotify's privacy policy applies: https://www.spotify.com/us/legal/privacy-policy/

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Services: Generate music, create videos, and upload content to social media platforms
  • Improve Services: Analyze usage patterns to enhance application functionality
  • Technical Support: Troubleshoot issues and provide customer support
  • Compliance: Meet legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • Credentials: OAuth tokens are stored locally in token.json files on your device
  • Generated Content: Music and video files are stored in the output/ directory on your local machine
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your files on our servers

4.2 Security Measures

  • Local Processing: All music generation and video creation happens on your local device
  • Encrypted Credentials: OAuth tokens are encrypted by the respective platform providers
  • No Data Transmission: Your music and image files are not transmitted to our servers

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize social media platform access
  • With Service Providers: Third-party APIs (YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Spotify) as necessary to provide services
  • For Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we have collected
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (see Section 7)
  • Correction: Correct inaccurate information
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the application and delete local files

7. Data Deletion

For detailed step-by-step data deletion instructions, please visit: Data Deletion Instructions

7.1 How to Delete Your Data

Local Files:

  • Delete the output/ directory to remove generated content
  • Delete token.json and credentials.json files to remove authentication data
  • Uninstall the application to remove all local data

Social Media Platforms:

Revoke access through each platform's settings:

  • YouTube: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Instagram/Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=applications
  • TikTok: https://www.tiktok.com/setting/privacy
  • Spotify: https://www.spotify.com/account/apps/

Request Data Deletion:

Email us at: neuralbeats20@gmail.com
Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
Include: Your name and any relevant account information

7.2 Data Deletion Timeline

  • Local Files: Immediately upon deletion
  • OAuth Tokens: Revoked immediately upon platform settings update
  • Third-Party Data: Subject to each platform's deletion policies

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are NOT intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is processed locally on your device. When you use social media APIs, your data may be transferred to servers located in different countries, subject to each platform's privacy policies.
